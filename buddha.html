Indian Mythology

Buddha Avatar

Lord Buddha is considered the ninth avatar of Vishnu in some traditions.

Born as Siddhartha Gautama, he renounced luxury to seek enlightenment. His teachings of non-violence, compassion, and detachment spread across Asia and the world. Buddha taught that suffering can be ended through the Eightfold Path.

This avatar reminds humanity to rise above desire and ego.
