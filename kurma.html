Indian Mythology

Kurma Avatar

The Kurma Avatar, or Tortoise Incarnation, is the second of the Dashavatara of Lord Vishnu.

In this form, Lord Vishnu supported Mount Mandara on his back to help the Devas and Asuras churn the cosmic ocean (Samudra Manthan) to extract the nectar of immortality (Amrit).

This avatar emphasizes support, balance, and cosmic duty — even divine beings must uphold dharma with patience and stability.
