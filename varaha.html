Indian Mythology

Varaha Avatar

The Varaha Avatar is the third form of Vishnu, taking the shape of a wild boar.

He appeared to rescue Mother Earth (Bhudevi) who had been submerged into the cosmic ocean by the demon Hiranyaksha.

With his tusks, Varaha lifted her back to the surface and restored balance in the universe.
