Indian Mythology

Krishna Avatar

Krishna, the eighth avatar, is both playful and profound.

From his childhood pranks in Vrindavan to his divine counsel in the Bhagavad Gita, Krishna is the most colorful of all avatars. He guided the Pandavas in the Mahabharata and played a key role in restoring dharma.

Krishna teaches love, wisdom, and strategic action.
