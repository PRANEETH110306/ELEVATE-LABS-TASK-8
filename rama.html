Indian Mythology

Rama Avatar

Rama is the seventh and one of the most revered avatars of Vishnu.

He is the hero of the Ramayana, known for his righteousness, devotion, and ideal conduct. Rama exiled himself to uphold his father’s promise and fought the demon king Ravana to rescue Sita.

His life exemplifies perfect manhood, leadership, and duty.
