Indian Mythology

Vamana Avatar

Vamana, the dwarf Brahmin, is the fifth avatar of Lord Vishnu.

He appeared to subdue the ego of the powerful king Bali. Asking for three steps of land, he then expanded into a giant form and measured the Earth and the heavens. For the third step, Bali offered his own head.

Vamana teaches humility and the power of devotion over pride.
