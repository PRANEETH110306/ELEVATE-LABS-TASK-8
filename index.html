The Four Yugas
  • Satya Yuga – Era of Truth, peace, and purity (100% Dharma).
  • Treta Yuga – Age of sacrifices and the time of Rama (75% Dharma).
  • Dvapara Yuga – Age of Krishna and Mahabharata (50% Dharma).
  • Kali Yuga – Present age of darkness and decline (25% Dharma).
